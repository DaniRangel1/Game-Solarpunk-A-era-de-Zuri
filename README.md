## 1. OVERVIEW

### üìò Project Summary
**Solarpunk: The Age of Zuri** is a third-person action-adventure game that combines narrative exploration, environmental puzzle-solving, and strategic battles against mechanical colossi.  

Set in a sustainable, post-apocalyptic future, the player follows **Zuri**, a young boy on a journey to find the legendary **Mother of the World** and uncover the truth behind his parents‚Äô disappearance ‚Äî in a planet rebuilt through harmony between nature and technology.

**üéÆ Genre:** Action / Adventure  
**üíª Platforms:** PC and Consoles (with future adaptation for Mobile)

## 2. PROJECT SCOPE

### General Description
The game takes place across six interconnected continents, each with distinct ecosystems, cultures, and technologies.  

Players freely explore these regions, solve environmental challenges, and face colossal robotic Guardians ‚Äî entities that preserve vital memories about Earth‚Äôs downfall and rebirth.  

The experience blends **immersive storytelling**, **reflective themes**, and **artistic expression**, exploring sustainability, technological ethics, and human empathy.  
The visual style draws inspiration from the **Solarpunk movement**, accompanied by a haunting, atmospheric soundtrack.

## 3. GAMEPLAY

### Core Mechanics
- Free exploration in an open, interconnected world  
- Global train system (*Treem*) as main transportation  
- Large-scale battles against mechanical colossi  
- Environmental puzzle-solving  
- Interaction with NPCs and moral decision-making  
- Progression system based on recovered ‚Äúmemory fragments‚Äù

### Combat Systems
Zuri uses three main combat tools:

- **Robot A (Defense):** Generates shields and absorbs damage  
- **Robot B (Strength):** Performs melee attacks and destroys obstacles  
- **Nanotech Glove:** Multifunctional weapon that can transform into a sword, shield, hook, energy cannon, or hacking device  

Players can dynamically switch between combat modes and use the environment tactically.

## 4. LEVEL DESIGN

### Level 1 ‚Äì Futuristic Rio de Janeiro  
Zuri begins his journey in a sustainable, vertical-green version of Rio.  
**Goal:** Gather tech parts to hack and upgrade his home robots, ‚ÄúMother‚Äù and ‚ÄúFather.‚Äù  
This serves as a **tutorial** for movement, parkour, and basic interactions.

### Level 2 ‚Äì The Intercontinental Train (*Treem*)  
Powered by oceanic energy, the Treem connects all continents.  
During a trip, a system failure gives the player 5 real-time minutes to investigate and interact with NPCs, introducing **dialogue and clue mechanics**.

### Level 3 ‚Äì The Mysterious Scientist  
Zuri meets **Vargas**, who claims to know his parents.  
He reveals that an AI called *Mother of the World* hides the truth about them.  
Zuri must choose to:
- **Trust Vargas:** Continue to the next continent  
- **Doubt Vargas:** Unlock new dialogue options in the previous area  

### Level 4 ‚Äì The Amazon Continent  
A ruined, polluted Amazon.  
Zuri builds a purifier and faces a **tree-like colossus**.  
Recovered memory: the start of the nuclear war and human greed.  

### Level 5 ‚Äì North America  
A desert wasteland of vertical farms.  
Zuri repairs agricultural drones and faces a **metallic sandworm colossus**.  
Recovered memory: creation of defense barriers and global arms race.  

### Level 6 ‚Äì Europe  
Ruined cities submerged in fog.  
Zuri faces a **metallic bullet-shaped colossus**, aided by his robots.  
Recovered memory: a man trying to destroy the nuclear barrier to stop the war.  

### Level 7 ‚Äì South Africa  
Frozen floating city.  
Zuri reactivates the **Solar Furnace** and faces a **lion-eagle-serpent hybrid Guardian**.  
Recovered memory: the barrier was destroyed, but too late to stop the apocalypse.  

### Level 8 ‚Äì Oceania  
A collapsing underwater city.  
Zuri collects components to restore oxygen balance and faces a **mechanical whale Guardian**.  
Recovered memory: early efforts to rebuild sustainable society.  

### Level 9 ‚Äì Asia  
Metropolis ruled by rebellious AI.  
Zuri learns Vargas has manipulated him into destroying the planet‚Äôs balance.  
He meets **Mother Earth**, the last AI, who reveals the truth about his parents.  

### Level 10 ‚Äì Final Confrontation  
Zuri faces Vargas in a symbolic digital chess duel, followed by a physical battle.  
If victorious, Zuri restores Mother Earth and begins a new era of harmony.

## 5. PROGRESSION & REWARDS

- Each Guardian defeated grants a **Memory Fragment** and new attributes:  
  - **Strength:** Increases physical attack  
  - **Defense:** Expands shields and resistance  
  - **Intelligence:** Unlocks advanced hacking and puzzle-solving skills  

- Unlockable upgrades:  
  - New Nanotech Glove transformations  
  - Expanded robot functionalities  
  - Inventory and secret area access

## 8. HISTORICAL CONTEXT

A thousand years ago, nuclear war wiped out 85% of humanity.  
Survivors built a **global society** founded on sustainability, justice, and individuality, supervised by **Mother of the World**, an AI maintaining balance between humans and machines.

---

## 9. MAIN STORY

**Zuri**, an orphan from futuristic Rio, lives with two reprogrammed robots ‚Äî *Mother* and *Father*.  
He sets off to uncover why his parents disappeared. Along his journey, Zuri gathers six **Memory Keys** hidden by colossal Guardians, each revealing fragments of Earth‚Äôs lost past.

### Central Conflict
A secret group led by **Vargas** believes equality is unnatural and seeks to restore the rule of the powerful.  
Zuri is unknowingly manipulated into dismantling the world‚Äôs balance.  
In the end, he learns the memories were not only about his parents ‚Äî but about humanity‚Äôs fate.
